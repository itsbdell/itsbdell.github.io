![Valedictorian](/images/projects/valedictorian.jpg)

A feature film, shot one day a month for a year. Each chapter captures a single day in the life of a recent graduate navigating the year after college.

The constraint — one shoot day per month — forced a documentary-like patience, letting real time shape the story.
