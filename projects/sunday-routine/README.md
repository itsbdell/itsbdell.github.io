# Sunday Routine

A collection of the weekend routines of interesting people.

## About

Sunday Routine explores how we find peace, order, and enjoy our lives. On the weekends.

This site features interviews with artists, writers, entrepreneurs, and makers, documenting their Sunday routines and what makes their weekends meaningful.

## The Interviews

The site includes 23 interviews with people from various creative fields:

- **Artists**: Musicians, singers, and visual artists
- **Foodies & Makers**: Chefs, recipe developers, and craftspeople  
- **Entrepreneurs**: Founders, designers, and business creators
- **Writers**: Journalists, authors, and creative writers

Each interview includes:
- A profile photo/thumbnail
- The person's name and title
- Their location
- Their full Sunday routine story

## Site Structure

- **Homepage** (`index.html`) - Categorized listing of all interviews with thumbnails
- **Interview Pages** - Individual profiles at root level (e.g., `adina-grigore.html`, `alec-bemis.html`)
- **Categories Page** (`categories.html`) - Organized listing by category
- **Images** (`images/`) - All interview photos and assets
- **Styles** (`css/`) - Site stylesheets

## Technical Details

- **Static Site**: Pure HTML/CSS, no build process required
- **Flat Structure**: All interviews at root level for clean URLs
- **Clean URLs**: Links without `.html` extensions
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Base Path**: Configured for `/projects/sunday-routine/` subdirectory hosting
