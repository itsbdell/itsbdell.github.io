## Who are you? What do you do for a living? 

<span style="font-size:15.555556297302246px">I'm </span><span style="font-size:15.555556297302246px">Laura</span><span style="font-size:15.555556297302246px"> </span>Burhenn and I have a band called [The Mynabirds](http://themynabirds.com/). I do that for a living, and I sometimes play in other people's bands. I toured with the Postal Service last year and Bright Eyes in 2011. These days I'm kind of a professional gypsy, having driven across America twice in the process of writing my latest Mynabirds record. Right now I'm en route to Nashville from Los Angeles. Go where the wild road leads, you know?
